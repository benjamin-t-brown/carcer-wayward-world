STR - weapon damage
  - Each point grants 1 additional base damage to melee weapons
MND - magic damage
  - Each point grants 1 additional base damage to magic spells
CON - health
  - Each point grants 1 hp per level
AGI - movement
  - Each point grants 1 ap
LCK - rolls
  - Each point raises the minimum of every dice roll that is made by 1d2 - 1

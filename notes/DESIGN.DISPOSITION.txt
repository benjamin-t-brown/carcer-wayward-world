Major characters who you can speak to have a Disposition stat, which represents their attitude towards you.

Disposition affects the following
- Locks/Unlocks dialogue options
  - Grants access to quests/areas
- Prices for buying and selling
- Their aggression towards you

Disposition can be altered on people by
- Being part of the same faction
- Having a personality aspect that vibes with them
- Picking certain dialogue options
- Doing stuff for them (quests)
- Having a high/low disposition for somebody else
NOTE: disposition is not affected by some weird mini game (Oblivion, Starfield)

Generic Combat Stats

STR - weapon damage
  - Each point grants 1 additional base damage to melee weapons
MND - magic damage
  - Each point grants 1 additional base damage to magic spells
CON - health
  - Each point grants 5 hp per level
AGI - movement
  - Each point reduces cost of actions by one
LCK - rolls
  - Each point raises the minimum of every COMBAT dice roll that is made by 1d2 - 1

** Trainable Combat Stats

Accuracy/Mastery: Each point grants +1 to attack roll
- Edged Weapons
  - one in each hand
  - high hit rate, B damage
- Pole Weapons
  - two-handed
  - lower hit rate, A damage
- Blunt Weapons
  - one or two handed
  - high hit rate, high range of damage C-A
- Range Weapons
  - simple ranged weapons scale linearly (ex 1d6 + <range training>)
  - complex ranged weapons scale exponentially (ex 1d<range training> + <range training>)
- Unarmed
  - can chain lots of hits
  - high hit rate, C damage

Skills Specific
Each level grants 5 points, thresholds are met at certain levels, capped at 25.  Advance training feat required for level 20, master training feat required for level 25.
- Trickery / Lockpick
  * Each level grants 1 less tool required to open locks.
  * Each 5th level grants bonus to sleight of hand checks (stealing/dialogue)
- Stealth
  * Each level increases amount of time it takes for someone to notice you while in stealth in their vision radius
  * Each 5th level decreases the vision radius of everyone on you by one
- Social
  * Each level grants 1% discount on buying TRADE goods.
  * Every 5th level grants a 10% boost of people's disposition towards you.
- Magic Item Use / Magic Affinity / Arcana / Mage Lore
  * Each level grants 1 higher threshold of learning arcane knowledge from runes.
  * Every 5th level doubles potency of magical items (potions, wands)
- Cooking
  * Each level reduces amount of food required to be satiated while traveling by 1%.
  * Every 5th level grants the ability to cook better food, which grants: more hp and higher disposition, resistance points? when a day starts.
- Acrobatics / Shortcut Usage / Terrain Traversal / Athletics
  * Each level reduces time taken to pass through over world tiles by 1 tick.
  * Every 5th level allows you to move an extra tile before costing AP
- Survival / Foraging / First Aid?
  * Every level grants 1% chance of finding alchemy or cooking ingredients when a day ends while traveling.
  * Every 5th level grants 1d4 per level higher health and mana gained after a fight concludes
- Focus
  * Every level grants 4 additional mana
  * Every 5th level grants 1 additional point to your highest combat trainable skill.
- Conditioning
  * Every level grants 1 additional HP when using a healing potion or item
  * Every 5th level grants a +1 bonus to all combat stats

Resistance Points:
  * 1 resistance point reduces damage to an aspect by 20% ONE time.  Then it is removed.

Feats (gained every 3 levels)
- Light/Heavy armor
- Fast Travel: More tiles traveled outside before days pass.
- Advanced Training: Can level up a skill up to 20 per skill
- Master Training: Can level up a skill up to 25 per skill (requires Advanced Training)
- Personal Space: You deal an extra 1d4 damage per 4 levels to enemies exactly 2 tiles away, counting diagonal.
- Inopportunist: You gain an additional approach attack while defending.
- Magical Efficiency: You use 25% less Sapphiron charge
- Healthy Specimen: Regular poison does not affect you, and you cannot be diseased
- Large Build: You are pushed 1 less distance if a force pushes you. (cant have small build)
- Small Build: You are pushed 1 further distance if a force pushes you. (cant have large build)
- Toughness: All damage dealt to you is reduced by 2, to a minimum of 1.
- Inner Calm: Gain additional maximum mana.
- Squib: Direct Sapphiron Magic is half effective on you, but you are half effective when using it.
- Assassination: When attacking an actor a lower level than you, you deal additional 1d4 per 4 levels true damage.
- Master Assassination: Assassination is calculated at 4 levels above your level.
- Homely: Almost everybody starts off with 0 disposition towards you.
- Crazy Critter: Criticals inflict a random status effect (positive or negative)
- Dodger: Your AC is increased by +2 permanently
- Ravenous Appetite: You eat twice as much food, but it provides double the effect.
- Quickstep: Your first movement per turn costs 0 AP.
- Seasoned Traveler: You can carry 2 more slots of rations.
- Device Illiterate: Sapphiron fragments don't always work for you, sometimes they do something random?
- Adrenalist: Defeating an enemy grants 1 AP.
- Groupie: Attacking an enemy who is adjacent to an ally has a +4 attack bonus.
- Sinister: Negative Status Effects last 1 turn longer per 4 levels.
- Ephemeral: Walking through fields is half effective against you.
- Hot: You have resistance to fire damage.
- Huskarl: You gain resistance to piercing damage.
- Armor Bearer: Wearing any armor grants 2 additional DR per level 
- Evasive: Your AC is raised by 2 permanently
- Mutant: You are resistant to radiation damage
- Burning Rage: While under effects of Burning or Inferno, your STR stat is doubled

ENEMY ONLY
- Poisoned Weapons: Your weapons always have poison applied
